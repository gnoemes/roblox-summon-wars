# Glossary v0.3

**Status:** Baseline glossary  
**Last updated:** 2026-03-08

---

## A

### Auto-use
System behavior where consumables trigger automatically based on fixed rules or buff absence.

---

## B

### Battle Stand / Combat Spot
The player's personal or semi-personal combat station inside a Region.

It acts as:
- combat access point;
- local guidance shell;
- social identity object.

### Bestiary
Account-level background progression system based on enemy **types**, not individual mobs.

### Body
Baseline defensive equipment slot.

### Booster
Monetized acceleration item that increases safe categories such as XP, basic resources, or base currency for a fixed number of wins.

### Boss Material
High-end resource family primarily obtained from Raids and major boss content.

---

## C

### Card
The item form through which fighters are obtained.

### Chest / Stash
Shared storage for **consumables only**.
Consumables auto-use directly from this storage.

### Class
The fighter's main role identity.

### Consumables
Preparation and support items used through auto-use logic.
Baseline categories: Food, Elixir, Potion.

### Core
Advanced resource family used for stronger recipes and advanced crafting.

---

## E

### Elixir
Long-duration party-wide consumable buff that auto-refreshes when absent and stocked.

### Encounter
One completed fight against one currently active target.

### Encounter Pad
The local stand anchor point where the current enemy manifests during combat.

### Enemy Combat Header
Compact enemy UI element that shows current combat state.
Baseline content: name, HP bar, tier badge, optional short threat tag.

### Extraction
Umbrella term for combat activity runs.
Baseline types: Hunting Grounds, Dungeon, Raid.

---

## F

### Fighter
Playable combat unit generated from a card.

### Fighters Panel
The fighter-management menu.
Baseline sections: Squad, Roster, Fusion, Synergy.

### Fighter Storage
Roster storage for owned fighters.

### Food
Long-duration party-wide consumable buff crafted mainly from Meat.

### Fusion
System where unwanted fighters are merged into a selected fighter to provide Ascension progress.

---

## G

### Guide Mascot
Support helper object attached to the stand.
Used for prompts and Guide access.

### Guide Panel
The stand support menu.
Baseline sections: Recommended, Tasks, Craft, Chest.

### Guided Reveal
Design principle where deeper systems are surfaced as solutions to visible friction and immediately useful next steps.

---

## H

### Hunting Grounds
Always-available Extraction type used for targeted low-friction farming.
HP fully resets between encounters.

---

## L

### Local Combat Mode
The temporary in-region combat-focused state entered after selecting a target.

---

## N

### Node
A fixed activity point inside a Region used for Dungeons, Raids, or other future content launches.

---

## P

### Portal Core / Reactor
The main central stand object that anchors combat visually and acts as the main battle interaction point.

### Potion
Situational consumable that auto-uses on a single fighter according to built-in conditions.

### Prestige
Visible status / identity value expressed through stand visuals, board style, titles, aura, and related presentation layers.

---

## R

### Raid
Gated high-end Extraction type with HP persistence, unique value, and strong preparation sensitivity.

### Recommended
The first Guide screen that surfaces the best current next actions for the player.

### Region
Compact social battle space that contains battle stands, node-based activities, and regional progression.

### Regional Guidance Chain
Short region-specific guided task chain used to reveal local mechanics and useful progression steps.

### Regional Progression
Primary meta progression system tied to playing content inside a specific Region.

### Resource Family
One of the broad grouped material types in the economy:
Meat, Herb, Ore, Core, Boss Material.

### Resource Inventory
Separate stack-based storage layer for resources.
Resources do not consume Chest slots in baseline design.

### Roster
Player-facing fighter list UI.
Preferred term over `Storage` for the fighter collection screen.

---

## S

### Social / Stat Board
Visible stand panel used for owner identity, public stats, and prestige display.

### Speed Food
Food type that increases Attack Speed / combat tempo.
Preferred player-facing replacement for Initiative Food language.

### Squad Power
Coarse power estimate used for recommendation and gating in Extractions.

### Storage
Monetizable and gameplay-relevant capacity layer.
In baseline design this mainly refers to:
- fighter roster slots
- chest slots

### Subclass
Specialization inside a class, derived from the generated special skill.

### Subscription
Recurring monetization layer focused on convenience, storage, and mild acceleration.

### Synergy
System where 10 cards are merged into 1 new randomized card.
Legendary results are excluded from baseline Synergy.

---

## T

### Target-click Combat
Combat model where the player starts combat by directly selecting a target.

### Tasks
Stand guidance layer consisting of regional chain steps and repeatable or rotating objectives.

### Timer / Interval Model
Combat timing shell based on attack intervals, cooldowns, and durations rather than visible round sequencing.

### Trio
The active 3-fighter combat team.

---

## Notes

This glossary intentionally focuses on system terms already fixed in the current baseline documents.
It is not yet a full lore, UI, or content glossary.
